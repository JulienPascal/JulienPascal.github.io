Labor Policy in a Dynamic Search-Matching Model with Heterogeneous Workers and Firms

Mar 14, 2019

We analyse the consequences of the minimum wage on employment and sorting in a model of the labor market with search frictions, heterogeneous workers and firms, and business cycle fluctuations. This is a joint project with Jeremy Lise and Jean-Marc Robin.
