Rental Housing Market and Directed Search

Introduction

Context

  • Rents have surged in big cities worldwide

The median American rent payment rose 61% in real terms between 1960 and 2016 while the median renter’s income grew by 5%

  • Housing shortages and rent control in many major cities

Questions

  • Are rent control legislations effective?
  • Do we observe asymmetric bargaining power between renters and landlords?

Data

Data Sources

  • Web scraping: online ads posted on the major platforms for the Paris market (PAP, Leboncoin, etc.) between April and May 2019
  • I observe the two sides of the rental housing market:
    • supply: apartment features, text description and photos
    • demand: number of contacts received by the renters
